Current Project

Identify Factors Leading to Popular Online News

Please find information about Current Project I am working on:

  • Factors Driven to Popular Online News

Links to our Poster and Report

In this project we mainly deal with identifing factors, such as publish day and number of words in tile, that will lead to online news with more shares. The dataset we use can be found at UCI Machine Learning repository. Distinguished fron previous research on this dataset, our main focus is not prediction, but the inverse problem instead. We want to develop a model with satisfiable accuracy but from which we can tell which factors or interaction of facotrs have more impact on the shares of online news.
